Seahorse

Seahorse males do something highly unusual in the animal kingdom: they get pregnant and deliver their offspring.

  • Scientific name: Hippocampus
  • Average size: 1.5 to 35.5 cm
  • Average lifespan: 1 to 5 years
  • Habitat: shallow tropical waters

Seahorse is the name given to 54 species of small marine fishes in the genus Hippocampus. Having a head and neck suggestive of a horse, seahorses also feature segmented bony armour, an upright posture and a curled prehensile tail.
